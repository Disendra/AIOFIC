About Us

The All in OTT Films & Industries Chamber of Commerce (AIOFIC) is a non-profit trade association that represents the interests of the over-the-top (OTT) film industry in India. This was founded in 2023 by a group of leading OTT players, including Netflix, Amazon Prime Video, Disney+ Hotstar, and Zee5.

The AIOFIC's mission is to promote the growth and development of the OTT film industry in India. The AIOFIC does this by advocating for policies that support the industry, working to resolve disputes between industry stakeholders, and providing a forum for members to collaborate and share best practices.

The AIOFIC also plays an important role in promoting Indian OTT films to international audiences. The AIOFIC works with international film festivals and distributors to showcase Indian OTT films at major events around the world.

In addition to its advocacy and promotional work, the AIOFIC also provides a range of resources and services to its members. These include market research data, legal advice, and training programs.

The AIOFIC is committed to working with all stakeholders in the Indian film industry to create a vibrant and sustainable OTT film ecosystem.
